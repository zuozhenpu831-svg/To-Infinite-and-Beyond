Physical Quantities Dictionary

物理量词典

Physical quantities (0.4x) describe the measurable aspects of motion and change in the universe. Unlike units (0.3x) which are fixed constants like Planck time or Planck length, these are variable quantities — concepts that any civilization studying motion must discover. 物理量（0.4x）描述宇宙中运动和变化的可测量方面。 与固定常数的单位（0.3x，如普朗克时间、普朗克长度）不同， 这些是可变的量——任何研究运动的文明都必须发现的概念。

Kinematic Quantities

运动学量

These quantities describe the geometry of motion — how objects move through space and time. Any civilization that observes moving objects must develop these concepts. 这些量描述运动的几何——物体如何在空间和时间中运动。 任何观察运动物体的文明都必须发展出这些概念。

Quantities vs. Units

物理量与单位的区别

Units (0.3x) are fixed reference scales — Planck time (0.31), Planck length (0.32), speed of light (0.301). They are constants that do not change.

Physical Quantities (0.4x) are the concepts being measured — time (0.43), displacement (0.42), velocity (0.41). They describe what varies in the universe.

For example: velocity (0.41) is measured in units of speed of light (0.301). Displacement (0.42) is measured in units of Planck length (0.32). Time (0.43) is measured in units of Planck time (0.31). 单位（0.3x）是固定的参考尺度——普朗克时间（0.31）、普朗克长度（0.32）、光速（0.301）。 它们是不变的常数。

物理量（0.4x）是被测量的概念——时间（0.43）、位移（0.42）、速度（0.41）。 它们描述宇宙中变化的事物。

例如：速度（0.41）以光速（0.301）为单位测量。 位移（0.42）以普朗克长度（0.32）为单位测量。 时间（0.43）以普朗克时间（0.31）为单位测量。
