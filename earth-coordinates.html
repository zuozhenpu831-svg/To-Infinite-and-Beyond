Earth Coordinates

地球坐标

How do you tell someone your address when there are no shared maps? You use the universe's own landmarks — pulsars for space, and the cosmic microwave background for time. 当没有共同的地图时，你如何告诉别人你的地址？ 你使用宇宙自己的地标——用脉冲星定位空间，用宇宙微波背景辐射定位时间。

"The cosmos is within us. We are made of star-stuff. We are a way for the universe to know itself."
— Carl Sagan

In the vast cosmic ocean, how does one describe "here"? Street names mean nothing. Planetary orbits are local artifacts. Even the position of our Sun within the Milky Way is meaningful only to beings who share our galactic neighborhood.

在浩瀚的宇宙海洋中，如何描述"这里"？街道名称毫无意义。 行星轨道只是局部产物。即使太阳在银河系中的位置， 也只对与我们共享银河邻里的生命有意义。

We need coordinates that transcend locality — that can be understood by any civilization, anywhere in the universe. We need cosmic lighthouses.

我们需要超越局部性的坐标——任何文明，在宇宙的任何地方，都能理解的坐标。 我们需要宇宙灯塔。

Why Both Space AND Time Coordinates?

为什么同时需要空间和时间坐标？

🌀 No Absolute Reference Frame

In the universe, there is no fixed "center" or absolute reference point. Everything is in motion: the Earth orbits the Sun, the Sun orbits the galactic center, and our galaxy moves through the cosmic web. A spatial position without a time reference is meaningless — the location you describe will have moved by the time someone arrives.

在宇宙中，没有固定的"中心"或绝对参考点。一切都在运动： 地球绕太阳运行，太阳绕银河系中心运行，我们的银河系在宇宙网络中移动。 没有时间参考的空间位置是没有意义的——当有人到达时，你描述的位置早已移动。

⏰ Pulsars Move Too

Even pulsars — our cosmic lighthouses — are not stationary. They have proper motions through space. The 15 pulsars we reference are identified by their unique rotation periods (which are extremely stable), not by their positions. But the distances from Earth change over time. By including the CMB temperature (our cosmic timestamp), we specify exactly WHEN these measurements were made.

即使是脉冲星——我们的宇宙灯塔——也不是静止的。它们有穿越空间的自行运动。 我们引用的15颗脉冲星通过其独特的旋转周期（极其稳定）来识别，而不是通过它们的位置。 但与地球的距离会随时间变化。通过包含CMB温度（我们的宇宙时间戳）， 我们精确指定了这些测量是何时进行的。

🎯 Complete 4D Address

Just as Einstein showed that space and time are woven together as spacetime, a complete cosmic address must include both. The 15 pulsars give us spatial triangulation; the CMB temperature gives us temporal context. Together, they form a complete 4-dimensional coordinate that any civilization can use to locate us in the universe.

正如爱因斯坦所展示的，空间和时间交织成时空，一个完整的宇宙地址必须同时包含两者。 15颗脉冲星给我们空间三角定位；CMB温度给我们时间背景。 它们共同构成一个完整的四维坐标，任何文明都可以用它在宇宙中定位我们。

What is a Pulsar?

什么是脉冲星？

A pulsar is a rapidly rotating neutron star — the collapsed core of a massive star that has died in a supernova explosion. As it spins, it emits beams of electromagnetic radiation like a cosmic lighthouse. Each pulsar has a unique "fingerprint": its rotation period, which can be measured to extraordinary precision.

脉冲星是一颗快速旋转的中子星——一颗在超新星爆炸中死亡的大质量恒星的坍缩核心。 当它旋转时，它像宇宙灯塔一样发射电磁辐射束。每颗脉冲星都有独特的"指纹"： 它的旋转周期，可以被测量到极高的精度。

Because pulsars are visible across the galaxy and beyond, and because their periods are unique and stable over millions of years, they serve as perfect cosmic beacons for triangulating position in space.

因为脉冲星在整个银河系及更远的地方都可见，而且它们的周期在数百万年内都是 独特且稳定的，所以它们是三角测量空间位置的完美宇宙信标。

Step 2: Universal Language Encoding

第二步：通用语言编码

Each pulsar is identified by its rotation period (in Planck time) and its distance from Earth (in Planck length). These universal units ensure that any civilization can reproduce the measurements. 每颗脉冲星通过其旋转周期（普朗克时间单位）和与地球的距离（普朗克长度单位）来识别。 这些通用单位确保任何文明都可以重现这些测量。

Temporal Position

The Universe's Clock

宇宙的时钟

Cosmic Microwave Background

宇宙微波背景辐射

Approximately 380,000 years after the Big Bang, the universe cooled enough for light to travel freely. That light — now stretched to microwave wavelengths by the expansion of the universe — fills all of space uniformly. This is the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB).

大约在大爆炸后38万年，宇宙冷却到足以让光自由传播。那些光——现在被宇宙膨胀 拉伸到微波波长——均匀地充满了整个空间。这就是宇宙微波背景辐射（CMB）。

The temperature of the CMB slowly decreases as the universe ages. Today it is approximately 2.725 Kelvin. This temperature provides a universal timestamp — any civilization measuring the CMB temperature can determine "when" they are in cosmic history.

CMB的温度随着宇宙衰老而缓慢下降。今天它大约是2.725开尔文。 这个温度提供了一个宇宙时间戳——任何测量CMB温度的文明都可以确定他们在宇宙历史中的"何时"。

Why CMB Temperature?

为什么用CMB温度？

🌌

Universal

The CMB is the same everywhere in the universe — any civilization can measure it.

CMB在宇宙的任何地方都是相同的——任何文明都可以测量它。

⏳

Time-Dependent

Unlike spatial coordinates, CMB temperature changes with cosmic time, marking "when."

与空间坐标不同，CMB温度随宇宙时间变化，标记"何时"。

📏

Precise

Can be measured to high precision, providing an accurate temporal anchor.

可以高精度测量，提供准确的时间锚点。
