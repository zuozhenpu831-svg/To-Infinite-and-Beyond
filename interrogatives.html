Interrogatives Dictionary

疑问词词典

Interrogatives (0.5x) extend the codebook from a system of statements into a system of questions and answers. This is an open series: the first published member is 0.51 (solve). Future members — for "why", "when", "whether", and others — are reserved. 疑问词（0.5x）将密码本从"陈述系统"扩展为"提问—应答系统"。 这是一个开放的系列：目前公开的第一个成员是 0.51（求解）， 未来将扩展"为何""何时""是否"等不同类型的疑问词，序号位为后续成员预留。

Earlier families (0.1x mathematics, 0.2x matter, 0.3x physics units, 0.4x physical quantities) teach the receiver how to describe the universe. The 0.5x family is what lets the receiver ask about it — and lets the sender embed verifiable answers in the same stream. This is the leap from a descriptive codebook to a conversational, self-verifying one. 前四个系列（0.1x 数学、0.2x 物质、0.3x 物理单位、0.4x 物理量）教接收者 描述宇宙；0.5x 系列让接收者可以提问，也让发送者把可验证的应答嵌进同一个序列。 这是从"描述式密码本"到"对话式、自验证密码本"的关键跃迁。

Reserved Slots

预留位

The 0.5x series is intentionally open-ended. Sub-codes 0.52, 0.53, … will be assigned to other interrogatives as the design matures — likely candidates include "why" (causal), "when" (temporal), "whether" (boolean inquiry), "where" (spatial), and "how-many" (quantitative). Receivers should treat any unrecognized 0.5x code as a question marker and look for the following payload + 0.9 terminator. 0.5x 系列刻意保持开放。后续 0.52、0.53… 将逐步分配给其它疑问类型， 候选包括"为何"（因果）、"何时"（时间）、"是否"（真伪）、"何处"（空间）、"多少"（数量）等。 接收者遇到未识别的 0.5x 编码时，应按"问句标记 + 载荷 + 0.9 结束符"的语法解析。

Query → Answer Grammar

提问与应答语法

A 0.51 question and its candidate answer are always paired with a truth verdict, so the whole exchange is auditable from a single transmission:

<equation> 0.9
0.51 <unknown> 0.9
<unknown> 0.15 <candidate> 0.18 <1 / 0> 0.9
Read line by line: state the equation, ask which symbol is the unknown, then assert the answer and mark it true (0.18 1) or false (0.18 0). Multiple answer lines can follow one query to teach the boundary between correct and incorrect solutions — the same self-explanation technique used throughout the codebook. 0.51 提问与候选答案始终与"真伪判定"成对出现，整段对话从单次传输内即可自证：
第一行陈述方程；第二行用 0.51 标出未知数；第三行给出候选答案，再用 0.18 加 1/0 标出正确或错误。 同一个问题可以挂多条答案行，用以划清正确解与错误解的边界——与其它密码本一致的自解释手法。

Why an Interrogative Series?

为何要引入疑问词？

A descriptive language can only assert. Once interrogatives exist, the receiver can verify that they have decoded the symbol set correctly — by checking that the sender's marked-true answers really do satisfy the equations and the marked-false ones don't. Comprehension becomes testable, not assumed. 纯陈述式语言只能"宣称"。一旦有了疑问词，接收者就可以反向验证自己是否解码正确—— 只需检查发送者标记为"正确"的答案是否真满足方程、标记为"错误"的是否真不成立。 理解从此可验证，而非默认成立。

The leap from answers to questions is the leap from broadcasting to conversation. With 0.51 in hand, the codebook stops simply describing the universe and starts inviting the receiver to check our work — and, eventually, to send questions back.

从"应答"到"提问"，是从单向广播迈向双向对话的飞跃。 握住 0.51 之后，密码本不再只是描述宇宙，而是邀请接收者验证我们的推理—— 并在未来某一天，把问题反过来抛给我们。
